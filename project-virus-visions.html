Virus Visions.

Virus Visions is a generative art project that uses Artificial Intelligence to create abstract interpretations of news articles about Brazilian President Jair Bolsonaro's handling of the COVID-19 pandemic. The project visually explores the chaotic and controversial responses through surreal and fragmented images, offering a unique perspective on the political and social impacts of the crisis.

The Project.

Overview

In 2020, with the early explorations of generative AI, I embarked on an art project utilizing VQGAN+Clip to create abstract images. At that time, platforms like DALL-E and Midjourney hadn't yet gained prominence, so my focus was on the abstract visual interpretations generated by these earlier AI models.

My primary exploration was to examine how these AI models visually interpreted news articles about the Brazilian president, Jair Bolsonaro, and his stance on the COVID-19 pandemic. The articles used for the prompts were sourced from a diverse range of media outlets, including The New York Times, BBC, The Guardian, Al Jazeera, CNN, Folha de São Paulo, The Economist, and others. The project initially started with news articles from 2020 but soon expanded to include articles from 2021 as well.

The AI-generated outputs comprised both images and videos, showcasing the process and evolution of the machine's interpretations.

Objectives

Visual Exploration: To explore the AI's capability to interpret and generate abstract visual representations of textual news articles.

Political and Social Commentary: To provide a visual commentary on the handling of the COVID-19 pandemic by the Brazilian president, as perceived through various international and national news outlets.

Generative Art: To create a body of generative art that merges technology, politics, and abstract visual aesthetics.

Process

Prompting AI: Using VQGAN+Clip in a Google Colab environment, I inputted text prompts derived from news articles.

Data Sources: Articles were selected from prominent news sources to ensure a broad and balanced perspective.

Generation: The AI generated abstract images and videos based on these prompts, which were then curated for the project.
