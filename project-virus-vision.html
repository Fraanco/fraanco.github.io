Brazil Virus Visions.

Brazil Virus Visions is a generative art project that uses Artificial Intelligence to create abstract interpretations of news articles about Brazilian President Jair Bolsonaro's handling of the COVID-19 pandemic. The project visually explores the chaotic and controversial responses through surreal and fragmented images, offering a unique perspective on the political and social impacts of the crisis.

The Project.

Concept

What would a post-apocalyptic world imagined by artificial intelligence look like? After the collapse of humanity as we knew it, the planet followed. Given the new circumstances, life returned to try, adapt and evolve. New microorganisms, plants, and animals redefining this new world. Not a single human being was left, but the machines learned the need to document.

Worldbuilding

QR Codex is a speculative evolution worldbuilding experiment that uses Artificial Intelligence to create a post-apocalyptic world where humans seized to exist because of a technology-related Event. We can follow the story through the pages of an encyclopedia that shows us and describe the creatures that inhabit this new world, and slowly presents us with how life is trying to thrive.

Format

The images are created with Midjourney, and the textual content (such as creatures’ anatomic descriptions, or original post-Event folk tales) is created with Open AI tools(such as Playground and Chat.ai).

During the first iteration, the project is being shared only through instagram, but the project's world will be expanded to other media, such as short stories, playing cards and an actual physical encyclopedia.
